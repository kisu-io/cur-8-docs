None of these block the 20-Apr construction start, but all need to be resolved within the first 2 weeks.

Design DNA

From Moodboards:

  • Material language: raw concrete + warm timber + brushed brass + wool acoustic panels
  • Color: oxblood, mustard, charcoal — warm low-light palette
  • Lighting: layered, dimmable, track + pendant + sconce; no fluorescent
  • Acoustic: Sound Lab treated to NC-20; PM bar treated to NC-30; cafe untreated
  • Signage: minimal, one wordmark, house serif used sparingly
