The building

49 Phó Đức Chính is a narrow shophouse: footprint ~17.18 m deep × 3.87 m wide (~66 m² per floor). Vertical circulation (single stair + lift) is stacked at the rear of the plan.

The program shift

The prior narrative (which was wrong) had: cafe on GF + 1F · bar on 2F · Sound Lab on 3F · BOH on 4F · lounge on rooftop. The real plan is: cafe on Trệt · Sound Lab on Lầu 1 · open-air lounge on Lầu 2 front · vinyl shop on Lầu 2 rear · office on Lầu 3 · kitchen/BOH on Lầu 4. No separate rooftop program.

Consequence — PM bar / lounge

The PM bar doesn't have its own dedicated floor. The open-air lounge on Lầu 2 front carries the PM drinks program. Seat count and bar footprint need to be resized accordingly — see 01_Planning/Business-Plan.

Consequence — kitchen on the top floor

Kitchen on Lầu 4 means deliveries (arriving at Trệt) ride the lift four levels to BOH. Plated food then travels down to Trệt (cafe), Lầu 1 (Sound Lab food service if any), and Lầu 2 (lounge). Service-lift capacity, speed, and redundancy become load-bearing. Hood / exhaust routing also needs a rooftop discharge path through/around the Mái slab.

Active seat count

Prior revenue model used ~100–125 "active" seats including a full bar floor and a rooftop deck. Neither exists. Seat counts by floor need to be recomputed against:

  • Trệt cafe (unchanged)
  • Lầu 1 Sound Lab (unchanged, bookable)
  • Lầu 2 front open-air lounge (new — size by the setback footprint)
  • Lầu 2 rear vinyl shop (retail, not seated revenue in the same way)

See 01_Planning/Business-Plan.

Access / circulation

  • Single stair core + single lift serve all floors (rear of plan, see DD sheet 102)
  • Lift is the accessibility path to upper floors AND the food service path to the cafe / lounge from the Lầu 4 kitchen
  • Service lift — referenced in prior wiki; existence + capacity not yet confirmed in the drawings. Becomes critical given kitchen is on Lầu 4.
  • Emergency egress: stair + rooftop escape route — PCCC package under preparation, see 04_Operations/Licenses

Acoustic zoning ⚠️ Under review

Previous acoustic targets were tied to floor assignments that don't match the real program. Acoustic zoning will be revised to the real stack:

  • Lầu 1 (Sound Lab) — NC-20 target, full isolation
  • Lầu 2 front (open-air lounge) — outdoor-grade, no NC target
  • Lầu 2 rear (vinyl shop) — needs quiet enough to audition records; isolate from lounge noise immediately in front
  • Lầu 3 (office) — quiet during work hours; sits directly over vinyl shop (and under kitchen)
  • Lầu 4 (kitchen) — noisy / hot / vibrating equipment; slab isolation between Lầu 4 and Lầu 3 (office below) is important

Open items to resolve with the architect

  1. Update the DD drawings to re-label the +11.600 level as Lầu 4 (enclosed kitchen) rather than Sân Thượng. Exterior elevations, section, and the floor plan for that level all need to change.
  2. Service lift — does it exist, and what is its capacity / speed? Kitchen on Lầu 4 makes this a critical spec.
  3. Kitchen exhaust routing from Lầu 4 through/around the Mái slab — rooftop discharge is required; the hood + make-up air design needs to accommodate this.
  4. Lầu 1' — what is the 550 mm split-level actually used for (raised rear deck on Lầu 1, stair landing, service void)?
  5. Weather protection for Lầu 2 front — open-air terrace needs a rain plan (overhang from Lầu 3? retractable awning? accept closure on heavy-rain days?).
  6. Vinyl shop adjacencies — hours of operation, inventory storage, customer flow vs. the open-air lounge immediately in front.
