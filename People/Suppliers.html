Supplier terms discipline

  1. 30-day terms minimum with core suppliers — protects cashflow
  2. No exclusivity unless it earns us 10%+ margin improvement
  3. Quality holds over price — wrong-quality deliveries get rejected, not reasoned
  4. Two sources for critical categories — one primary, one backup, always
  5. Quarterly supplier review — bottom 20% replaced unless relationship-critical

Relationships to nurture

  • Apero + Bar Lead — our cocktail program's raw material quality lives here
  • Vietnamese roaster + Kitchen/Bar Lead — our coffee identity lives here
  • Cellier D'asie + GM — wine program's depth and access to allocations
  • Local produce + Kitchen Lead — plate margin and seasonality lives here

Opening-specific

Pre-opening purchases (first 30 days stock): - ~300M VND initial inventory - Covers: backbar fill, beer + wine cellaring, first 2 weeks produce and proteins - Included inside the 1.19B working capital line
