Team principles

  • Two leads protect the revenue: Bar Lead (cocktail margin + PM revenue) and Kitchen Lead (plate margin + cafe food program). Both get profit share.
  • GM holds the room: service standards, hiring, culture. GM's weekly meeting with both leads is the operating rhythm.
  • Published schedule 3 weeks ahead. Non-negotiable for retention.
  • Two days off per week per employee. Non-negotiable.
  • Internal promotion path. Every FTE has a named next step if they want it.

Key person risks (from ../04_Operations/Reports/01_Risk-Analysis)

  • Bar Lead loss = revenue risk; cocktail program is irreplaceable in <30 days
  • Kitchen Lead loss = menu integrity risk
  • GM loss = operational continuity risk

Each of these 3 hires has a named "90-day succession sketch" in the GM's private file.
