Business Plan

Parent: Home · Source: 01_planning/business_plan.html · Related: Finance-Model, Unit-Economics, 00_Overview/Concept

Concept summary

CUR8 is a multi-concept hospitality venue: a specialty cafe by day (Trệt), a cocktail bar and open-air lounge by night (Lầu 2 front, under the Lầu 3/4 setback), a dedicated Sound Lab (Lầu 1), and a vinyl shop (Lầu 2 rear). Office on Lầu 3, kitchen/BOH on Lầu 4. One address, several programs, fourteen-hour operating window.

See 00_Overview/Concept for the full positioning narrative.

⚠️ The revenue model tables below still reflect the earlier program (including a "rooftop weekend" segment as a separate pillar). Under the 2026-04-20 restack the "rooftop" crowd is absorbed into the PM open-air lounge on Lầu 2 front. Numbers need re-basing — tracked in Home pending-revision list.

Revenue model

The model assumes two distinct revenue regimes:

  • Sun–Wed (4 days): 100 active seats · 160K avg spend · slower turn — cafe-led
  • Thu–Sat (3 days): 125 active seats · 700K avg spend · faster turn — bar-led

Implied weekly revenue: ~327M VND. Implied monthly: ~1.215B VND. Annualized: ~14.6B (rounded down to 13.83B in the model to account for ramp + seasonality).

⚠️ 06_Financial-Advisory flags this as 20–35% optimistic. The advisory base case lands at ~12.5B Y1.

Operating model

  • Trệt + Lầu 1–4 (5 occupied programmatic levels + Lầu 1' split; no separate rooftop program — open-air lounge is on Lầu 2 front, kitchen/BOH on Lầu 4); see 02_Design/Floor-Layout
  • 18-person staff (full + part-time blend); see 04_Operations/Shifts
  • 8 supplier relationships core (Apero, Jay, Con Rồng, Alchemy, Cellier D'asie, Mega Market, etc.); see People/Suppliers
  • 3 shift codes (AM, PM, Late); see 04_Operations/Shifts

Differentiation

Lever What we do Why competitors can't copy easily
Sound Lab 12-seat dedicated listening room, tube amplification, vinyl curation Requires acoustic build-out + equipment investment. Jackalope, Stir, Mizuwari, Kanpai don't have one.
Daypart density Same room earns rent 14h/day Most local cafes fade after 16:00; most bars don't open until 18:00
Vietnamese audiophile angle Local beans + global cocktail program + curated music Most "listening bars" in HCMC are Japanese pastiche

Risks acknowledged in plan

  1. Revenue assumptions are top-down (see 04_Operations/Reports/01_Risk-Analysis)
  2. Capex risk if construction blows past V2 (~4B) — see 03_Construction/Construction-Budget
  3. Talent risk for bar lead and Sound Lab curator
  4. Concentration risk on Thu–Sat revenue (~60% of week)
  5. Regulatory / licensing delays could slip opening past June

See 04_Operations/Reports/01_Risk-Analysis and 06_Financial-Advisory for full risk treatment.
