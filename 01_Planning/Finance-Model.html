Revenue build (Y1)

  • Sun–Wed: 4 days × 100 seats × 160K = 64M/day × 4 = 256M/week
  • Thu–Sat: 3 days × 125 seats × 700K = 87.5M/day × 3 = 262.5M/week
  • Weekly total: ~518.5M × 52 weeks = ~26.9B gross before ramp, seasonality, and realization

The model discounts this heavily to arrive at 13.83B Y1. The discount factor is effectively ~52% — which is aggressive even before accounting for the 6-month ramp that 06_Financial-Advisory wants added.
