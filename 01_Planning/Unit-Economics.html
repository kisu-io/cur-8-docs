Unit Economics

Parent: Home · Related: Business-Plan, Finance-Model, 04_Operations/Reports/03_Daypart-Simulation

Per-seat economics (planned)

Daypart Active seats Avg spend / seat-turn Turns / seat / day Implied rev / seat / day
Sun–Wed cafe-led 100 160K 1.0 160K
Thu–Sat bar-led 125 700K 1.0 700K
Sound Lab evening 12 800K 1.0–1.5 800K–1.2M

The model treats each seat as turning roughly once per day. That is a strong assumption — see 04_Operations/Reports/03_Daypart-Simulation for a stress test.

Average check architecture

Check tier Composition Target % of checks
Cafe AM (low) 1 coffee + small item — ~120K 35% of AM
Cafe AM (mid) Coffee + lunch — ~220K 50% of AM
Cafe AM (group) 2–3 people, food + drinks — ~400K 15% of AM
Bar PM (low) 1–2 drinks — ~280K 25% of PM
Bar PM (mid) Drinks + plates — ~550K 50% of PM
Bar PM (high) Tasting + Sound Lab — ~900K 25% of PM

Blended Thu–Sat target: ~580K — model uses 700K. Gap: 120K = ~17%. This is the avg-check optimism flagged in 06_Financial-Appendix (missing) — see 06_Financial-Advisory.

Per-hour economics

Hour band Open hours Seats sold / hour (target) Revenue / hour
10:00–12:00 2 25 ~3M
12:00–14:00 2 50 ~7M (lunch peak)
14:00–16:00 2 30 ~3.5M
16:00–18:00 2 40 ~10M (early bar)
18:00–22:00 4 90 ~50M (peak bar)
22:00–00:00 2 60 ~25M (late bar)

Implied daily peak (Thu–Sat): ~98M. Implied daily off-peak (Sun–Wed): ~28M.

Contribution margins

Category Revenue mix COGS % Contribution %
Coffee & cafe drinks 15% 18% 82%
Cafe food 10% 35% 65%
Cocktails 35% 25% 75%
Beer / wine 15% 38% 62%
Bar plates 15% 32% 68%
Sound Lab cover & extras 5% 15% 85%
Rooftop / events 5% 25% 75%

Blended contribution: ~72%. Model assumes ~70%. Acceptable.

Three numbers to monitor weekly

  1. Average check Thu–Sat (target 580K, model claims 700K)
  2. Seat-turns per seat per day (target 1.0, advisory says watch closely)
  3. Cafe AM bill count (target 120 bills/day Sun–Wed) — early indicator of cafe traction
