Field visit log — template + index

Parent: Competitor Landscape. One file per venue, named YYYY-MM-DD__<venue-slug>.md.

Cadence: HIGH-threat venues re-visited quarterly; MED every 6 months; LOW once per year.

Why this exists

Revenue estimates and threat ratings on Competitor-Landscape are extrapolated from public data. Field visits are how we convert extrapolation into evidence. Each visit should be short (30–60 min), repeatable, and photographed so anyone on the team can audit it later.
