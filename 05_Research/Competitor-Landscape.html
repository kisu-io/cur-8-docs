🎯 White Space Opportunities for CUR8

1. "Day-to-Night" — Unclaimed Territory

R.A.C and Carpe Diem attempt this model but neither is dominant. CUR8 can own this positioning with stronger execution (Sound Lab + dedicated programming).

2. Retail + F&B Integration — No One Does This Well

No venue in the area sells products (merch, specialty goods, vinyl). CUR8 can add retail revenue stream on top of F&B.

3. Community / Event Space — "Third Place" Gap

Home Pub has Phở Đặc Biệt brand but lacks third-place vibe. CUR8's Sound Lab + programming can fill this void.

⚠️ Competitive Risks

1. Over-saturated café market: Katinat + Là Việt already capture morning/lunch crowd. CUR8 needs differentiation in afternoon/evening slots.

2. Night competition is fierce: Home Pub, FETTI, The Arth are established. CUR8 needs clear identity to compete for PM crowd.

3. Rent pressure: District 1, near major intersection = premium rent. AM revenue helps fixed cost recovery but margin pressure is real.

🎯 Strategic Recommendations

Factor Strategy
Differentiation Hybrid café-bar + retail (merch, products) — not just drinking, also buying. Sound Lab is unique asset.
