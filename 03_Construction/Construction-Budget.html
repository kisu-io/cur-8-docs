Construction Budget

Parent: Home · Source: 03_construction/ · Related: BOQ, Construction-Timeline, Payment-Schedule

Budget bands

Scenario Construction % of capex (4.15B)
V1 (base) 2,710M 65%
Mid-case working number ~3,160M 76%
V2 (upgrade) 4,010M 97%

The mid-case (V1 base + Sound Lab acoustic upgrade + deferred rooftop premium) is the realistic working number. It fits inside the 4.15B capex envelope with ~990M of headroom for FF&E, contingency, and over-runs.

Tied to payment milestones in Payment-Schedule.

What's excluded from this budget

  • FF&E (furniture, loose items) — in capex, not construction
  • 04_Operations/Equipment (bar tools, kitchen equipment, AV) — in capex, separate line
  • Licenses + permits — pre-opening budget, see 04_Operations/Licenses
  • Pre-opening marketing — pre-opening budget

Discipline

One line that must hold: contingency is not spent first. Many HCMC fit-outs burn the contingency in month 1 on "unexpected" structural conditions. Our 180M PM+contingency is sized tight; any structural surprise needs a pause-and-decide meeting before it gets spent.
