Payment Schedule

Parent: Home · Source: 03_construction/payment_schedule.xlsx · Related: Construction-Budget, Construction-Timeline

Structure

Milestone-based, tied to physical completion with retention held against snag list.

Milestone Description % of contract Estimated VND (mid-case) Trigger
1 Mobilization + deposit 10% 320M Contract signing + site secure
2 Demolition + structural reinforcement 15% 480M Engineer sign-off on structural
3 Services rough-in (elec, plumb, HVAC) 20% 640M MEP coordinated sign-off
4 Walls + ceilings framed (incl Sound Lab) 20% 640M Acoustic engineer sign-off on Sound Lab partition
5 Finishes + joinery installed 15% 480M Joinery install complete, lighting working
6 FF&E + commissioning 10% 320M Functional test + PCCC pre-inspection
7 Snag complete + handover 5% 160M Zero open items on snag list
Retention Held 3 months post-open 5% 160M Released 3 months after hard open
Total 100% ~3,200M Per Construction-Budget mid-case

Controls

  • No payment without written milestone sign-off from GM + project manager + relevant engineer (structural/MEP/acoustic).
  • Change orders issued separately with clear scope + price + impact on timeline; no verbal change orders; budget owner signs.
  • Retention of 5% is non-negotiable — this is what gets the snag list closed.
  • Direct payment to the main contractor only; sub-payments are the main contractor's responsibility.
  • Penalty clause for delay: 1M VND/day past Jun 18 opening, capped at 2% of contract value (~64M).
  • Bonus clause for early delivery: 0.5M/day pre-Jun 18, capped at 1% (~32M).
