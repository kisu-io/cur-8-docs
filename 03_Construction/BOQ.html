BOQ — Bill of Quantities

Parent: Home · Source: 03_construction/BOQ_*.xlsx (V1, V2) · Related: Construction-Budget, Payment-Schedule

Two scenarios on the table

Scenario Total Profile
V1 — base ~2,710,000,000 VND Functional fit-out; standard finishes; no upgraded acoustic package; rooftop awning basic
V2 — upgrade ~4,010,000,000 VND Premium finishes; full acoustic package including Sound Lab isolation; rooftop premium awning + planting

Delta: ~1,300M VND. Most of the delta lives in three line items:

  1. Sound Lab acoustic build-out (~+450M)
  2. Premium finishes across PM bar + Sound Lab (~+500M)
  3. Rooftop weatherproofing + landscape (~+250M)
  4. FF&E premium (~+100M)

Reconciliation status

🟡 Open as of 2026-04-20. No version is signed yet.

The recommended path (under discussion): - Adopt V1 as the base for cash management - Add V2 acoustic package only for the Sound Lab (~+450M) — non-negotiable for the differentiator - Defer rooftop premium to a Y1 reinvestment if revenue clears plan - Result: ~3,160M mid-case construction

This mid-case is what the Construction-Budget page treats as the working number.

Risks

  • Vietnamese construction inflation running ~6–8% YoY for finishes
  • Sound Lab spec drift — once acoustic engineer reviews, scope may grow
  • Hidden conditions at the existing building — likely contingency >5% needed
  • PCCC inspection can require change orders — budget for a 2nd round
