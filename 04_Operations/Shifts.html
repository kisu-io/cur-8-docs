Shifts

18-person roster · 3 shift codes

Shift codes

Code Hours Coverage
AM 09:30 – 17:00 Open, cafe service, transition
PM 15:30 – 00:30 Transition, bar service, close of bar
Late 19:00 – 01:30 Peak bar + close

Overlap: 15:30–17:00 is the transition band where AM closes and PM opens — the room reconfigures and the bar counter takes over.

Daily staffing targets

Sun–Wed (lower-volume days)

Shift Staff on floor
AM 1 cashier, 1 server, 1 barista (cross-trained), 1 commis, 1 housekeeping
PM 1 bartender, 1 server, 1 commis, 1 security, supervisor or GM

Thu–Sat (peak days)

Shift Staff on floor
AM 1 cashier, 2 servers, 1 barista, 1 commis, 1 housekeeping
PM Bar lead, 2 bartenders, service lead, 2 servers, 1 commis, 1 security, GM
Late 2 bartenders, 2 servers, 1 security

Peak Thu–Sat evening head count on floor: 9+ during 19:00–22:00.

Rotation philosophy

  • Two-off-per-week rule. No employee works 6 days in a row.
  • Sundays closed at 00:30 (not 01:30). Team gets a reasonable Monday.
  • Late shift never paired with AM shift next day. Minimum 10h rest.
  • Peak rotation is Thu–Fri–Sat. Everyone signs up knowing Thu–Sat is the job.

Labor cost mapping

Shifts produce the ~336M/month labor line in OpEx. Target: - Labor as % of revenue: 28% steady-state - Peak Thu–Sat labor: ~85 person-hours/day across all shifts × 3 days - Off-peak Sun–Wed labor: ~55 person-hours/day × 4 days
