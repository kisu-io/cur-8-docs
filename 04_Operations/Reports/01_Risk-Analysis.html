Risk categories

Revenue risks

  • Realization: model assumes ~58% of theoretical max daily; actual Q1 may be 40–50%
  • Check size optimism: 700K Thu–Sat vs blended 580K target
  • Ramp speed: model says 2–3 months, advisory says 6 months
  • Weather: rooftop exposure

Cost risks

  • Labor over-hire (most common F&B failure mode)
  • COGS drift from bar prep inconsistency
  • Utilities summer premium
  • Construction cost overrun → reduces working capital buffer

Regulatory / licensing risks

  • PCCC (highest schedule risk)
  • ATVSTP kitchen layout dependency
  • Music + public performance licensing
  • Rooftop curfew enforcement

Talent risks

  • Bar lead attrition
  • Kitchen lead attrition
  • Part-time pool turnover
  • Training cost vs retention

Market risks

  • Competitor opening (Kanpai-style venue in District 1) — see ../../05_Research/Competitor-Landscape
  • Tourist flow seasonality
  • Expat population churn

Tripwires (from ../../06_Financial-Advisory)

  1. Month 3 revenue < 50% of model → pause capex, freeze hiring
  2. Month 6 EBITDA negative → bridge funding conversation
  3. Month 9 revenue < 75% → restructure
  4. Construction cost > 110% of budget → freeze FF&E premium
  5. 2 license rejections → operational pause, owner-level meeting
  6. Key person loss (Bar Lead or Kitchen Lead) in first 90 days → emergency hiring protocol
