Fixed vs variable

  • ~405M fixed opex is what the venue burns every month regardless of revenue.
  • Break-even revenue = 405M / (1 − 0.30 COGS) ≈ ~580M/month ≈ ~19M/day.
  • Sun–Wed Block B alone (~24M/day) covers the fixed cost; Thu–Sat is pure margin territory.

Opex discipline points

  1. Labor is the lever. Over-hiring in month 1 is the classic F&B mistake. Ramp labor to revenue (see Reports/04_HR-Rampup).
  2. Marketing budget is intentionally thin. The plan is to let the room and word-of-mouth carry the opening. Don't let this balloon above 2% of revenue without a specific campaign reason.
  3. Utilities will spike in April–October (aircon). Budget an extra 20% summer premium.
  4. Music licensing is non-negotiable for a listening-concept venue — do not shortcut.
