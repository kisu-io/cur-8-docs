Margin architecture

Blended target margins: - Coffee: 78% - Cafe food: 60% - Cocktails: 73% - Beer/wine: 62% - Bar plates: 62% - Sound Lab: 75%

Blended ~70% gross margin → 30% COGS → matches model assumption.

Menu discipline rules

  1. No item below 55% margin. Period.
  2. Cocktail menu: 12 signatures + 6 zero-proof + classics on request. Don't expand.
  3. Bar plates: 8 items + 1 rotating. Kitchen capacity caps the menu.
  4. Cafe food: 8 items. Lunch bowls flex daily.
  5. Pricing reviewed quarterly, never reactively to a single bad week.
